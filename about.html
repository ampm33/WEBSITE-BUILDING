Bio

Hello! I am a creative specializing in photography, with over 5 years of professional experience. I focus on making images that feel grounded, intentional, and emotionally honest.

I am taking this class to strengthen my technologic and aesthetic skills, while also continuing to build my foundation in creative and business disciplines.

  • Clean structure and intentional creative decisions
